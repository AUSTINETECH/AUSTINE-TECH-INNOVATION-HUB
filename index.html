Our direction

Practical technology. One useful build at a time.

Agriculture is still full of manual work, scattered records, slow communication, and missed market information. We design digital tools around those real problems instead of chasing technology for show.

The team is already shipping public prototypes, portfolio builds, and Vercel deployments that show consistent product practice. Our goal is to grow into a serious Kenyan technology studio with the discipline and ambition to compete far beyond our first market.

What we are

Austine Tech Innovation Hub is a Kenyan technology studio built by three founders with roots in agricultural economics, extension science, and software engineering. We are not a consulting firm. We are builders.

What drives us

Agriculture remains underdigitized. Scattered records, slow communication, missed market signals. We see that gap and build toward it — with tools designed around what farmers and traders actually need, not what impresses investors.
